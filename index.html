Dr. Asma Ibrahim Al Mannaei is one of the Arab world's most distinguished healthcare executives — a physician, policy architect, and innovation catalyst whose work has fundamentally elevated Abu Dhabi's position as a global centre of healthcare excellence.

As Executive Director of the Research and Innovation Centre at the Department of Health – Abu Dhabi, Dr. Al Mannaei leads the emirate's agenda to accelerate healthcare research, assess adoption of breakthrough innovations, novel medications, and transformative technologies across the UAE health system.

With over 18 years at the forefront of public health, strategy, quality governance, and research — she has authored landmark frameworks, incubated first-of-their-kind regional programmes, and shaped the future of an entire nation's healthcare ecosystem.

Leadership Philosophy

Sustainable healthcare transformation demands the courage to innovate, the rigour to measure, and the humanity to listen. Dr. Al Mannaei's leadership bridges science and governance, research and practice, local context and global ambition.
